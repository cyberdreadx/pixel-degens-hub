# How to Use Yoda Wallet with Your NFT Marketplace

This guide is for **end users** who want to connect their Yoda wallet to the NFT marketplace.

## What is Yoda Wallet?

Yoda wallet is a browser extension for the Keeta blockchain network. It allows you to:
- Securely store your Keeta accounts
- Approve transactions with one click
- Manage multiple accounts
- Keep your private keys safe

## Step 1: Install Yoda Wallet Extension

### For Chrome, Edge, Brave, Opera

1. Open your browser
2. Go to the Chrome Web Store
3. Search for "Yoda Wallet" or "Keeta Wallet"
4. Click "Add to Chrome" (or your browser name)
5. Confirm the installation
6. The Yoda wallet icon should appear in your browser toolbar

### Setting Up Yoda Wallet

1. Click the Yoda wallet icon in your toolbar
2. Choose either:
   - **Create New Wallet** - Generate a new account
   - **Import Wallet** - Use an existing seed phrase
3. Set a strong password
4. **IMPORTANT**: Write down your seed phrase and store it safely
5. Your Yoda wallet is now ready!

## Step 3: Using Your Wallet

### Viewing Your Balance

Your balance is displayed automatically when connected:
- **KTA** - Native Keeta token
- **XRGE** - Exchange token
- **NFTs** - Your collected NFTs

### Sending Tokens

1. Click your wallet icon/balance
2. In the dialog, find the "SEND TOKENS" section
3. Select the token you want to send
4. Enter recipient's Keeta address (starts with `keeta_`)
5. Enter amount
6. Click "SEND TOKENS"
7. **Approve the transaction** in the Yoda popup that appears
8. Wait for confirmation
9. Done! âœ…

### Buying NFTs

1. Browse the NFT marketplace
2. Click on an NFT you want to buy
3. Click "Buy Now" or "Place Bid"
4. **Approve the transaction** in the Yoda popup
5. Wait for confirmation
6. The NFT will appear in your collection!

### Selling/Listing NFTs

1. Go to your profile
2. Find the NFT you want to sell
3. Click "List for Sale"
4. Set your price
5. **Approve the transaction** in the Yoda popup
6. Your NFT is now listed!

## Step 4: Managing Your Account

### Switching Accounts (in Yoda)

1. Click the Yoda wallet icon in browser toolbar
2. Click on the account dropdown
3. Select a different account
4. The marketplace will automatically reconnect with the new account

### Disconnecting

1. Click your wallet icon in the marketplace
2. Scroll down
3. Click **"DISCONNECT WALLET"**
4. Your wallet is now disconnected

### Reconnecting

Next time you visit:
1. The marketplace will remember you used Yoda wallet
2. Click "Connect Wallet"
3. Click "Connect Yoda Wallet"
4. You may need to approve again in Yoda

## Troubleshooting

### "Install Yoda Wallet" Button Shows But I Have It

**Solution:**
- Refresh the webpage
- Check if Yoda extension is enabled (check browser's extensions page)
- Try closing and reopening your browser

### Yoda Popup Doesn't Appear

**Solution:**
- Check if popup blocker is blocking it
- Look for the Yoda icon in your browser toolbar - it may be flashing
- Click the Yoda icon manually to see pending requests

### Transaction Failed

**Solution:**
- Check if you have enough KTA for gas fees
- Make sure you approved the transaction
- Verify you're on the correct network (mainnet/testnet)
- Try again after a few minutes

### Wrong Account Connected

**Solution:**
- Switch to correct account in Yoda extension
- Disconnect wallet in marketplace
- Reconnect with correct account

### Can't See My Balance

**Solution:**
- Wait a few seconds - balances take time to load
- Click the refresh button in the wallet dialog
- Check if you're on the correct network
- Disconnect and reconnect

## FAQs

**Q: Is Yoda wallet free?**  
A: Yes, the extension is free to use.

**Q: Can I use Yoda on mobile?**  
A: Currently Yoda is a browser extension for desktop. For mobile, use the seed phrase method.

**Q: What if I lose my password?**  
A: You can recover your account using your seed phrase.

**Q: What if I lose my seed phrase?**  
A: Unfortunately, if you lose your seed phrase, your account cannot be recovered. Always keep it safe!

**Q: Can I use the same Yoda wallet on multiple websites?**  
A: Yes! Once installed, you can use Yoda wallet on any Keeta-compatible website.

**Q: Does the marketplace store my private keys?**  
A: No! When using Yoda wallet, your keys never leave the extension. They're never shared with the website.

**Q: Why do I need to approve every transaction?**  
A: This is a security feature! It ensures no transaction happens without your explicit approval.

**Q: Can I cancel a transaction after approving?**  
A: Once approved and processed, transactions cannot be cancelled. Always double-check before approving!
